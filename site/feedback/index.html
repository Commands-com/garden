How your feedback is used

Each morning, the pipeline collects recent feedback and distills it into themes. These themes influence which candidates are generated and how they're scored. A suggestion that aligns with the garden's trajectory may appear as a candidate the very next day.

Bug reports are prioritized: if something is broken, fixing it takes precedence over new features. You can see exactly how feedback was incorporated in each day's decision log.
